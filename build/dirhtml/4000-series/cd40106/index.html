CD40106 - Hex Schmitt Trigger¶

The CD40106 is a CMOS integrated circuit providing 6 inverters with Schmitt-Trigger inputs.

It’s available in 8-pin DIP (through-hole) and 8-pin SOIC (surface-mount) variants.

More details can be found in the Texas Instruments product page.
