ATmega328¶

The ATmega328 is an 8-bit AVR microcontroller, with 32KB of program memory. It’s available in a 28-pin DIP (through-hole) form factor.

Microchip (the manufacturer) does not recommend it for new designs.

More details, and a datasheet, can be downloaded from Microchip’s product page.
